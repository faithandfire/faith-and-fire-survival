Why Support This Work?

Faith & Fire Survival exists to strengthen faith, build resilience, and equip families with practical preparedness rooted in biblical stewardship.

Many of our online resources are offered freely, and in-person ministry training is provided using a mission-based cost recovery model whenever possible.

Your support helps sustain and expand this work so more churches, families, and communities can benefit from practical, Christ-centered training.

“Carry each other’s burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ.” — Galatians 6:2

How Your Support Helps

  • Training materials and equipment upkeep
  • Travel expenses for ministry events
  • Website and resource development
  • Future training opportunities and outreach
