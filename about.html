About the Ministry

We unite biblical teaching with practical survival skills—equipping people to serve with wisdom, courage, and compassion.

Our Story

Faith & Fire Survival began with a simple conviction: preparedness is stewardship. As families and churches asked how to respond to uncertainty with peace instead of panic, this ministry formed to train both the heart and the hands.

We combine Scripture, leadership, and essential skills—so that individuals and families can be ready to care for one another and serve their communities in times of need.

What Makes Us Different

  • Faith-first approach: Every skill ties to a biblical principle.
  • Family-friendly training: Practical for all ages and skill levels.
  • Service mindset: Prepared people are freed to serve others.
  • Safety & wisdom: We emphasize discernment, not fear.

Statement of Faith

Scripture
