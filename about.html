Why We Started Faith & Fire Survival

Faith & Fire Survival was born out of a conviction that true preparedness goes deeper than physical skill. In a world growing more uncertain, we saw a need for training that strengthens both the body and the spirit.

Too often, survival education focuses solely on gear and technique, while faith is treated as separate or secondary. We believe Scripture teaches otherwise.
