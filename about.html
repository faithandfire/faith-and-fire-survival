Our Team

Faith & Fire Survival LLC is led by two experienced outdoor survival instructors passionate about teaching practical wilderness skills rooted in Biblical principles.

Derek Haber

Derek brings more than 25 years of expertise in wilderness survival, navigation, search & rescue, and emergency preparedness. His approach integrates proven survival techniques with timeless biblical principles, equipping individuals to thrive while fostering faith, resilience, and purpose.

Andy Bell

Andy is an accomplished outdoor educator specializing in hiking, backcountry camping, and wilderness survival. He mentors students in both technical skills and spiritual growth, helping them develop confidence in the outdoors and in life.
